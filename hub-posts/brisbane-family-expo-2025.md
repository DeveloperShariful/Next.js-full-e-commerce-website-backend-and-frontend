The GoBike team had an absolute blast at this year's Brisbane Family Outdoor Expo! We set up a massive test-ride track, and the turnout was incredible. 

If you have ever wondered what makes us the leading provider of **kids ebikes in Australia**, watching hundreds of kids jump on our bikes and instantly find their balance tells the whole story.

### Why Test Rides Matter
Buying a **kids electric bike** is an investment in your child's outdoor development. At our events, parents get to see firsthand:
* **The Sizing:** Kids could test the 12-inch, 16-inch, and 20-inch models side-by-side to find their perfect fit.
* **The Safety Features:** Parents loved feeling the fully enclosed chain guards and testing the responsive disc brakes.
* **The Instant Confidence:** It was amazing to watch kids who were nervous at first, zooming around the track within five minutes!
