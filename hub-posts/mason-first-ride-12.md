For parents wondering if their toddler is ready for an **electric balance bike**, Mason is the perfect example of how intuitive and safe these bikes are.

### Building Confidence from Day One
The GoBike 16 is designed specifically as an entry-level **kids ebike in Australia**. Here is why parents love it for their toddlers:
* **Freewheel Mode:** Mason started with the power OFF, using it as a traditional push balance bike to learn the basics.
* **Lightweight:** Weighing just enough to be stable but light enough for a 3-year-old to handle easily.
* **Low Speed Setting:** Once Mason was balancing, his parents switched it to the lowest speed mode (approx 8km/h) for a gentle, safe push.

Say goodbye to training wheels. The GoBike helps develop core strength, hand-eye coordination, and spatial awareness faster than traditional pedal bikes.
